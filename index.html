Frequently Asked Questions

What is Cliparoo?

Cliparoo is a clipboard manager that allows you to save and organize your clipboard history.

How does Cliparoo work?

Cliparoo automatically captures clipboard content and stores it as entries. You can view, organize, and interact with these entries.

What types of content can Cliparoo handle?

Cliparoo currently supports text-based content and may expand to support other formats like images or files in future updates.

Does Cliparoo save clipboard history between sessions?

Yes, Cliparoo uses localStorage to persist clipboard entries, ensuring your history is available even after closing the app.

How do I reset my clipboard history?

You can reset your clipboard history by selecting the "Reset Data" option in Cliparoo's menu, clearing all saved entries.

Is Cliparoo secure?

Cliparoo values your privacy and stores all data locally on your device. No clipboard data is sent to external servers.

What operating systems does Cliparoo support?

Cliparoo is a desktop application built with Tauri, making it compatible with Windows, macOS, and Linux.
